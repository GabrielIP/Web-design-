Mission Shredpossible

New York City and its surrounding areas have a rich skateboarding history. Although originating on the West coast, New York City quickly became one of the most sought after skateboarding locations. If your're new to the area there is a steep learning curve but this site aims to provide information for locals and visitors alike.

This site will rank diffrent skateparks with a highly scientific method that I personally created. Taking into key factors of the skateparks the skill required, condition ,and complexity will be evaluated. The rating for the three cateregories are Nah, Meh and Dope. Nah is the lowest ranking, while Meh is chillin in the middle somewhere and Dope is the highest.

Be sure to check out the skate spots on the right for some dope links with plenty of reviews pulled from yelp. All the skatepark banners have some steezy links with even more info as well.

Check out the site and go shred!
