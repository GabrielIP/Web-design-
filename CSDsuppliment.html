Pier 62 Skatepark

"My favorite place to skate in NYC. Always a solid crew skating here. Great flow section, nice bowl with pool coping. The coping in the shallow end is REALLY chunky so you really need to plow through it."-Paul P.

Hips, bowls, snake runs... What else can u ask for? This isn't a street spot (for that, skate on down to Tribeca.. it's also off the west side highway path a bit south of canal street) but they have 2 ledges, but u gotta hit them and keep it moving, or another ripper will end up landing on u! Definitely not a beginner spot! This spot is also not a place for spectators to go inside and watch (there's a sign outside the park that says no spectators). Yea, that ledge you are sitting on... that's a skate obstacle. People wanna nose grind, 5-0 grind, flip trick every way possible in and outta that thing... So if u wanna watch, keep it outside the barred fence! Sure, most skater's won't tell you that you're being a dick, they'll just make fun of you or talk shit about u with ur friends. I might say something though ;) but really, I might! If u gotta be in there when there's people skatin, at least stand by the fence! -Vicky P.
